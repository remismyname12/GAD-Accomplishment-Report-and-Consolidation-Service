I. ACTIVITY PROFILE

 

Title

BSU, Ondaw shi naybetaw (BSU Going the Distance)

Purpose

1.    To celebrate the university charter anniversary of BSU

2.    To provide opportunity for employees to highlight their camaraderie and sportsmanship, and showcase their skills

3.    To present a healthy pastime to employees so they may opt to refrain from vices such as drinking, smoking, etc.

4.    To provide valuable learning to employees such as on teamwork, healthy competition, and coping under pressure.

5.    To provide avenue for outreach program through sharing of school supplies to be contributed by participants

II. RATIONALE with GAD Perspective

 

It is an objective of the university to enhance and develop employee welfare programs (Goal II, Objective III, BSU VMGO). It is thus inherent for the university to be of assistance in ensuring the health and wellness of employees. This is by providing opportunity for physical fitness, and health, wellness and safety program. One of which is through an organized Fun Ride.

 

With the Section 14 in the Magna Carta of Women highlighting to develop, establish and strengthen programs for the participating of women and girl-children in competitive and non-competitive sports as a means to achieve excellence, promote physical and social well-being, eliminate gender-role stereotyping, and provide equal access to the full benefits of development for all persons regardless of sex, gender identity and other similar factors.

 

Mobility is an essential element of development strategies that aim to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Meeting the needs of people who cycle continues to be a critical part of the mobility solution for helping population growth from increased emissions, and to improve air quality and road safety.

 

This could be an amazing experience for number of employees in celebrating the Charter Day Celebration to join taking the paved roads, beautiful trails, breaking out daily routine, getting more active, fitter and healthier. Not only body will lose a few pounds, but the process will boost your immune system, relieve stress and feed you with an extra dose of endorphins. Besides the sense of achievement and satisfaction, there is a purpose for completing the race and get the feel of winning.

 

III. OBJECTIVES

 

The objectives of this program are:

1.    To organize a fun ride where female and male employees, students, alumni, and the public in general will be able to participate.

2.    Choose from among female and male employees athletes who can compete in regional competitions such as the ASCU-SN Olympics
